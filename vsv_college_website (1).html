🏛️ About VSV

A Legacy of Academic Excellence in Kundapura

Varasiddhi Vinayaka Pre-University College, nestled in the serene village of Keradi, Kundapura, has been a beacon of quality education in the Udupi district. Our college is committed to nurturing the intellectual, moral, and physical development of every student.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty of over 200 educators, and a curriculum aligned with PUC Board Karnataka, we offer an environment where students thrive academically and personally.
